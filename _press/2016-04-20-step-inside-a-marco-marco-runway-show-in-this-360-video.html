--- layout: post title: Step Inside a Marco Marco Runway Show in this 360 Video date: 2016-04-20 ---

L.A. label Marco Marco has always boasted exciting runway shows thanks to a habit of using gay icons, drag queens and porn stars to show off its latest designs – and now it’s found a way to let everyone share the experience: 360 VR video directed by Jason Koffeman, Executive Produced by Kevin Cornish. “Virtual reality lets viewers feel an experience never before possible,” said Moth + Flame VR’s Kevin Cornish. Marco is a true visionary, so what better way to bring the rush of his latest inspiration to the world than through this visionary new medium?”
